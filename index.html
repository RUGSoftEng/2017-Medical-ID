Medical ID

In an emergency, it's important that you're able to get the medical treatment you need urgently. It may be the case that you are on your own in a foreign country, or simply out by your self; if you need assistance, then it's important your medical details are accessible quickly.

With MedID, your urgent medical information will be on you at all times. If you're incapable of giving the information, someone will be able to immediately see your urgent details and get you the help you need.

Guest session

If you prefer not to sign up, you can still create a document or card as a guest. Doing this will mean none of your data is stored online. However, you will not have a personal profile, nor will you be able to easily edit your document in the future.
